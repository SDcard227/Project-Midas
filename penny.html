How It Works

THE DAY TRADING CYCLE

Every 60 seconds during market hours, Midas runs this cycle on every ticker in your watchlist.

01 // SCAN
READ 5-MIN BARS

Downloads the last 50 five-minute candles for each ticker. Computes tight EMA crossovers (3/9, 5/13, or 9/21 depending on frequency setting).

02 // SCORE
FUSE ALL SIGNALS

Combines EMA crossover, RSI position, news sentiment, and YouTube coverage into a 0–100 confidence score. Only high-confidence setups trigger a buy.

03 // EXECUTE
BUY, RIDE, EXIT

Buys on strong signals. Sells on bearish reversal or RSI overbought. Hard force-close of all positions 10 minutes before market close — no overnight holds ever.

Layer 5 — YouTube

CATCHING BREAKOUTS
AS THEY GO VIRAL

Penny stocks move because of attention — social media, news coverage, Reddit. Midas scans YouTube in real time for new video content on each ticker. A stock getting sudden coverage is a signal in itself.

The scanner runs two separate searches — one for stocks you already hold, one for new movers showing signs of life.

SECTION 1 — POSITIONS
  • Scans every ticker in your current open positions
  • Searches: "[TICKER] stock today", "[TICKER] earnings", "[TICKER] news"
  • Detects sentiment shift — if a held stock goes negative, triggers early sell review
  • Protects against holding through a news-driven drop
SECTION 2 — NEW MOVERS
  • Scans watchlist tickers not currently held for new coverage
  • Searches: "[TICKER] breakout", "[TICKER] squeeze", "[TICKER] going up"
  • A sudden spike in video count = attention signal before price moves
  • Feeds confidence score as Layer 5 to weight buy decisions higher

Intraday EMA Settings

TIGHT SIGNALS FOR
FAST MARKETS

5-minute bar EMA periods are much tighter than swing trading. The goal is to catch momentum in the first 30–60 minutes of a move, not ride a multi-week trend.

NORMAL // 9 / 21
~45 MIN / ~105 MIN

Moderate sensitivity on 5-min bars. ~45 minutes of price history for the fast EMA, ~105 minutes for the slow. Fewer signals, more conservative. Best when the market is choppy.

ACTIVE // 5 / 13
~25 MIN / ~65 MIN

Tighter crossovers. Catches moves earlier than NORMAL. The recommended default for penny day trading — fast enough to get in near the start of a breakout without overtrading.

AGGRESSIVE // 3 / 9
~15 MIN / ~45 MIN

Tightest crossovers available. Gets in very early on breakouts but generates the most false signals. Best when combined with a strong YouTube or news signal for confirmation.

PATTERN DAY TRADER RULE

The SEC requires a $25,000 minimum account balance to make more than 3 day trades in a 5-day window on US equities. This is the PDT rule.

In paper mode, the PDT rule does not apply. Alpaca's paper trading environment lets you simulate day trading with any balance, unlimited times. Test the system fully before committing real money.

RISK DISCLOSURE

Penny stocks are extremely volatile. Prices can move 30–80% in a single session in either direction. Midas forces all positions closed before market end — but intraday losses are still possible. Always start in paper mode (PAPER_TRADING = True in main.py). Never risk money you cannot afford to lose. This is not financial advice.
