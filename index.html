Strategy Modes

HOW AGGRESSIVE
DO YOU WANT TO BE?

Mode controls when Midas exits a position. Pick based on your risk tolerance and the market environment. You can change it any time and rerun the backtest to compare.

CLIMB
// Default · Balanced risk/reward

The standard mode. Midas rides the EMA trend and exits when the trend breaks down. Sells on a bearish EMA crossunder, RSI overbought (>70), or negative sentiment. Balanced — captures most of the move without being too greedy.

SELL triggers:
→ Bearish EMA crossunder
→ RSI climbs above 70 (overbought)
→ News sentiment turns negative

PLATEAU
// Conservative · Early exits

Gets out early. If price goes sideways for 10+ days within a 2% range, Midas takes profit before the sideways turns into a drop. Uses a larger re-entry dip (8% vs 4%) because flat-to-down moves tend to overshoot on the way back.

SELL triggers:
→ Price moves sideways for 10+ days (plateau detected)
→ All CLIMB sell triggers also apply

Trade Frequency

HOW OFTEN
SHOULD MIDAS BUY?

Frequency controls when Midas enters a position. Mode and frequency work independently — any mode can be paired with any frequency. AGGRESSIVE + SURGE is the highest-conviction, highest-risk combination.

NORMAL
Fewest trades · Highest confidence

Only buys on a confirmed EMA bullish crossover — the fast EMA crossing above the slow EMA — while RSI is below 60 and sentiment is not negative. Every signal has to be clean. Fewer trades, but higher quality per trade.

EMA 12/26 bullish crossover confirmed
RSI below 60 at time of entry
Sentiment not negative
Best for: steady trends, lower activity tolerance
ACTIVE
Moderate trades · Catches dips

Adds RSI dip-buying on top of NORMAL crossover entries. When RSI dips below 40 while the overall trend is still up, Midas buys the pullback. Catches mid-trend corrections that NORMAL would sit out.

Everything NORMAL does, plus:
EMA 5/13 (tighter periods)
RSI dip below 40 while trend is up → BUY
Best for: most situations, default recommendation
AGGRESSIVE
Most trades · Trend riding

Buys whenever the fast EMA is above the slow EMA — no crossover required. Stays in the position throughout an entire uptrend rather than waiting for crossovers. Also catches RSI dips below 40. Highest trade count.

EMA 5/13 — buy whenever fast > slow AND RSI ≤ 65
No crossover required — rides the full trend
RSI dip below 40 also triggers a buy
Best for: strong bull runs (2021, 2023)

Capital Protection

FOUR WAYS TO
PROTECT YOUR MONEY

The floor is a hard minimum. If the fund ever falls to it, Midas exits everything and stops trading automatically. Choose how that floor behaves.

FIXED
// Simple hard stop

Set a dollar amount. If the fund hits that number, trading stops. The floor never moves — simple tripwire. Good starting point for new accounts.

$0FLOOR $800$1,000
Best for: getting started, simple setups
TRAILING
// Ratchets up with gains

The floor rises as the fund grows — always trailing X% below the all-time peak. Locks in gains automatically over time. The floor never falls, only rises.

$0FLOOR $1,113PEAK $1,310
Best for: active trading, locking in long-term gains
LOCKED
// Floor in cash, never touched

The floor amount is kept in cash and never invested. Midas can only trade with capital above the floor. Mathematically impossible to reach the floor through normal trading.

$800 locked·$200 deployable
Best for: protecting a fixed reserve, trading gains only
PRINCIPAL
// Starting balance locked forever

Original investment is locked in cash permanently. Midas only ever trades with profits above the starting balance. Zero downside risk on your initial money.

$1,000 locked·gains only
Best for: zero-risk deployment, trading house money

Re-Entry System

SELL HIGH.
BUY BACK LOWER.

After every sell, Midas doesn't immediately jump back in. It places a watcher on the stock and waits for the post-sell peak to form, then catches the next dip. Avoids chasing momentum at the top.

01
SELL
Signal triggers. Position closes. Proceeds split: 75% reserved for re-entry, 25% stays liquid.
02
WATCH
Watcher placed. Tracks every price update. Watcher expires after 60 days if never triggered.
03
PEAK
Price rises above the sell price — confirming a new local peak. Watcher now active for dip detection.
04
DIP
Price drops 4% below the post-sell peak (8% in PLATEAU mode). Dip threshold crossed.
05
RE-ENTER
75% of original proceeds deployed automatically. Watcher removed. Position reopened.

4%

PLATEAU Mode
