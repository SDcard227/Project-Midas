What we collect

Account info you give us: email, your real first and last name, an optional nickname and bio, and optionally your country and state for leaderboards. Content you create: comments, room messages, watchlist tickers, private direct messages, who you follow, Parlor activity, and any comics/artwork you upload. Basic technical data: a session cookie and your IP, used for rate-limiting and abuse prevention. If you subscribe, payment is handled by our payment processor (we do not store card numbers).

How we use it

To run your account, show your posts and reputation, deliver your direct messages, manage follows and mutuals, place you on leaderboards, personalize your feed, and keep the platform safe (rate limits, abuse review). We do not sell your personal data.

Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in. It is HttpOnly and SameSite. No third-party ad tracking.

Messages & uploads

Direct messages are private between participants but are not encrypted; we may review them if reported for abuse. Uploaded images are reviewed before they appear publicly. We keep your data while your account is active; ask us to delete it and we will, except where we must retain records for legal or safety reasons.

Your choices

You can edit your nickname and notification preferences in Settings. To delete your account or data, contact us (add a real email before launch).

Third parties

We call market-data and AI providers (Alpaca, Finnhub, FMP, Anthropic) to power signals and dossiers, sending them tickers and headlines, not your personal account details. If you subscribe, our payment processor (Stripe) handles billing and receives the payment details you give it directly. Our hosting provider stores the platform's data.
