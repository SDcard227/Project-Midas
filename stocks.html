    Strategy Modes

    PICK YOUR APPROACH

    Three modes control when Midas exits a position. Set in main.py or via py setup.py → [4] Trading.

    CLIMB
    Default. Rides the EMA trend and exits on a bearish crossunder or RSI overbought. Balanced risk/reward. Best for most situations.
    PLATEAU
    Exits early when price goes sideways before a drop. More conservative — gives up some upside to avoid getting caught in a slow bleed.
    SURGE
    Holds the longest. Only exits when the peak is actively breaking down. High conviction — captures the full move but carries more drawdown risk.
    Capital Protection

    FLOOR PROTECTION

    Set a minimum balance. If the fund ever drops to that number, Midas exits all positions and stops trading — protecting your capital automatically.

    • FIXED — hard floor, trading stops if fund hits the limit
    • TRAILING — floor rises as your fund grows, locks in gains
    • LOCKED — floor amount kept in cash, never invested
    • PRINCIPAL — original investment locked forever, only trades profits

    Getting Started

    SET UP IN 4 STEPS

    STEP 01 // API KEYS
    CONNECT ALPACA

    Run py setup.py → [2] API Keys. Enter your free Alpaca paper trading keys from alpaca.markets. No real money needed to start.

    STEP 02 // WATCHLIST
    ADD YOUR STOCKS

    Run py setup.py → [6] Watchlist. Add any NYSE/NASDAQ ticker — AAPL, NVDA, SPY, or anything you want to track.

    STEP 03 // FUND
    SET YOUR BALANCE

    Run py setup.py → [3] Fund. Set your starting balance, floor amount, and how much to deploy per trade (default 10%).

    STEP 04 // LAUNCH
    START TRADING

    Double-click start.bat → [5] Trade. Midas runs in paper mode by default — watch it trade with fake money before going live.

    Trade Frequency

    HOW OFTEN DOES IT BUY?

    Independent from mode — controls how tight the entry conditions are. Pair with any trading mode.

    NORMAL
    Strictest filter. Only buys on a confirmed EMA bullish crossover while RSI is below 60 and sentiment is clean. EMA 12/26 periods. Fewest trades — every one is high conviction.
    ACTIVE
    Everything NORMAL does, plus: buys RSI dips below 40 while the overall trend is up. EMA 5/13 periods. Catches mid-trend pullbacks that NORMAL would sit out. Recommended default.
    AGGRESSIVE
    Buys whenever fast EMA is above slow EMA — no crossover required. Stays in the full uptrend. Also catches RSI dips. EMA 5/13. Most trades. Best in strong bull years like 2021 and 2023.
    Smart Re-Entry

    SELL HIGH.
    BUY BACK LOWER.

    After every sell, Midas doesn't immediately chase back in. It places a watcher on the stock, waits for a confirmed post-sell peak, then triggers a re-buy when price dips 4% from that peak.

    • 75% of sell proceeds reserved for re-entry
    • Remaining 25% stays liquid as a buffer
    • Watcher expires after 60 days — funds returned if no dip
    • PLATEAU mode uses 8% dip threshold (flat-to-down moves overshoot)
    • Multiple watchers can run simultaneously across tickers

    Cost & Tax Model

    REAL NUMBERS.
    NO SURPRISES.

    Transaction costs and taxes are deducted at execution — not estimated after the fact. Every reinvestment and compounding calculation uses real after-friction capital.

    TRANSACTION COSTS
    commission$0.00 per side
    spread_pct0.03% per side
    round-trip cost0.06% of trade value

    Commission = $0 because Alpaca is commission-free. Spread of 0.03% (3 bps) is a conservative estimate for liquid large-caps like AAPL, MSFT, NVDA, and SPY.

    TAX MODEL
    methodshort-term capital gains
    rate22% (configurable)
    appliedat sell, trade-by-trade

    Conservative model — treats every gain as short-term income regardless of hold time. Tax deducted at sell execution, so reinvestment amounts reflect real after-tax proceeds. Losses get no tax benefit (conservative).
